History of Independence

India's Independence Day is celebrated every year on August 15th, marking the end of British rule in 1947. This day commemorates the sacrifices made by countless freedom fighters who fought for the nation's liberty. Independence Day is a reminder of India's struggle for freedom, led by iconic leaders like Mahatma Gandhi, Jawaharlal Nehru, and Sardar Patel, who united the nation for a common cause.
